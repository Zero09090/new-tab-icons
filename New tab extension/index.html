Quickly backup to your Google Drive. Requires an OAuth Client ID applied to manifest.json.

How to Setup Google Drive Sync ⚙️
  1. Turn on Developer Mode in chrome://extensions and copy this extension's 32-character ID.
  2. Go to the Google Cloud Console and click New Project.
  3. Go to APIs & Services > OAuth consent screen. Select External, fill required fields, save, and hit PUBLISH APP on the summary page.
  4. Go to Credentials > + Create Credentials > OAuth client ID.
  5. Select Chrome app, paste your extension's 32-character ID, and hit Create.
  6. Copy the generated Client ID.
  7. Open this extension's manifest.json file, paste your Client ID into the oauth2 section, save the file, and hit Reload in chrome://extensions.
